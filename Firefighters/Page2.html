Education Required

To become a firefighter you need to be atleast 18 years old and have to have your highschool diploma. If you have those you must then pass a physical test, a written test. a drug screening exam, and a medical exam. You also have the chance to go to a school that teaches you learn specific skills necesary to being a firefighter. A lot of candidates have a degree in fire science when they apply for the job. Most firefighter recruits get their job training at firefighter academies which usually takes several weeks to complete the program that teaches them basic things about firefighting and potential dangerous materials.
