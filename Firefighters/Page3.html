There are different ranks that make different amounts of money and the location you work at also depend on how much you get paid. The average firefighter makes about $46,870 a year. The lowest 10% of firefighters earned $23,010 and the top 10% earned 79,490. California has some of the higher salary ranges where some of them earn $78,580-$89,840. This number can also vary depending on how much overtime you work. The average firefighter earns around $40,000, the avearge lieutenant earns around $55,000, the average captain earns around $60,000, and the average fire chier earns about $70,000. A firefighter can earn paid vacation time, training and education reimbursements, and sick leave. They also have the option to have an early retirement and are only required to serve for 20 years and the pension varys depending on location and additional years of service.
