Fire fighters have multiple purposes and skill sets for their jobs. They have to respond to chemical spills, water rescues, emergencies, and fight fires. They have to repond fast to calls or something bad can happen to people in the surrounding areas. They have to go into a lot of dangerous situations and have to be careful and make sure they are doing the right things so nothing goes wrong. They also have to be quick when they get a call and put on all of their equipment as fast as possible and quickly leave the station.
