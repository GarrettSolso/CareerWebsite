What You Actually Do As A Firefighter

Previous Next

Firefighters usually respond to emergency situations, respond to and put out fires, and control fires. Firefighters also have to respond to situations where chemicals are putting people or the environment at risk. When they aren't responding to fires they have to make sure their station stays clean and they also have to constantly clean their vehicles and equipment. They also have to make sure all of their equipment necesary to their jobs is in the right spot and is in working order so they don't run into any problems while out on the job.
